How It Works

From Your Request to Your Warehouse

No experience needed. Tell us what you want — we take care of everything from factory research to final delivery.

01

Submit Your Request

Share product details, target quantity, and budget. We respond with a sourcing plan and estimated cost within 24 hours.

02

We Find the Right Factory

We identify and vet 3–5 qualified suppliers. You get a comparison report with real pricing — no hidden markups.

03

Sample & Confirm

We arrange samples from your chosen factory. Once you approve, we lock the order and manage all communication.

04

Production & QC

We follow up during production, conduct pre-shipment inspection, and send you photos and a full QC report.

05

Shipping & Customs

We arrange sea, air, or express shipping. Export docs and customs clearance handled. Tracking provided throughout.

06

After-Sales Support

We stay on until you're satisfied. Any quality issues are taken up with the factory — we protect your interests.
